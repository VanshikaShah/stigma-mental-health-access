Data & Methods

  • Data: Healthy Minds Study (web-based survey of college student mental health and help-seeking)
  • Measures: PHQ-9, GAD-7 severity categories; perceived need; past-year treatment; barrier selections
  • Derived metrics: “Unmet need” = moderate-to-severe symptoms + no treatment
  • Notes: Cross-sectional, self-reported; possible selection and response bias
